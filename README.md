# online_store

Introduction
Welcome to our simple online store! This project, developed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and ASP, provides a user-friendly platform for browsing and purchasing products. As a user, you can explore the available items, add them to your cart, and complete the checkout process.

Key Features
Home Page:

Displays the name and a brief description of the online store.
Presents a list of the available products, including the product name, price, and a small image.
Product Detail Page:

Allows you to view detailed information about a selected product, such as the product name, description, price, and a larger image.
Provides the option to add the product to your shopping cart.
Shopping Cart:

Enables you to manage the items in your cart, including adding, adjusting quantities, and removing products.
Displays the product name, quantity, and price for each item in your cart.
Checkout Page:

Shows the total cost of the items in your cart.
Allows you to enter your shipping information.
Includes a button to complete the purchase.
Getting Started
Access the Online Store:

Open your web browser and navigate to the URL of the online store.
Browse and Explore:

On the home page, you can browse the available products and their details.
Click on a product to view its detailed information on the product detail page.
Add to Cart:

From the product detail page, you can add the item to your shopping cart.
Manage Your Cart:

Access your shopping cart to review the items you have added.
Adjust the quantities or remove items as needed.
Proceed to Checkout:

When you're ready to complete your purchase, click the "Proceed to Checkout" button.
On the checkout page, review the total cost and enter your shipping information.
Click the "Complete Purchase" button to finalize your order.

The Backend works with the checkout page to save the user's information.
